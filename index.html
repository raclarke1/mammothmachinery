OUR FLEET

Built Tough. Ready to Rent.

From powerful excavators to versatile skid steers — all Caterpillar, all maintained to the highest standards.

Excavators

Mini to full-size excavators for digging, trenching, demolition, and site prep. Complete with hydraulic hammer, auger, and compaction plate attachments.

Track Dozers

Powerful CAT dozers for serious earthmoving, land clearing, and grading. Built to handle Utah's toughest terrain with precision control.

Skid Steer Loaders

Multi-purpose workhorses with 20+ attachment options — augers, brooms, brush cutters, grapple buckets, cold planers, trenchers, and more.

Wheel Loaders

Reliable loaders for material handling, stockpiling, and loading trucks. Fork and bucket attachments available for every job.

Backhoe Loaders

The ultimate versatility machine — dig, load, and handle materials with one piece of equipment. Extra buckets, hammers, and compactors available.

Scrapers & Graders

Large-scale earthmoving and precision grading equipment for road construction, site development, and large commercial projects.

Compaction Equipment

Walk-behind rollers, vibratory smooth drum and pad foot rollers for soil compaction, asphalt work, and trench backfill.

Cranes & Forklifts

Lifting and material handling solutions for construction, warehouse, and industrial applications. Multiple capacities available.
