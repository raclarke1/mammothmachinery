GETTING STARTED

Rental Guide

Four simple steps to get the equipment you need on your job site.

01

Credit Application

Download the Credit Application to set up your Mammoth customer account. We'll review your information and inform you of your approved payment terms. Email the completed form to accounting@mammothmachinery.com.

Download Credit Application
02

Master Rental Contract

Download and review the Mammoth Master Rental Agreement, which includes all rental terms and conditions. Completing this up front will expedite all future rentals.

Download Rental Agreement
03

Certificate of Insurance

We require a certificate of insurance before equipment leaves our yard. You have two options:

Option 1: Damage Waiver

19% of gross rental charge. $5,000 deductible.

Option 2: Your Own Insurance

Commercial General Liability ($1M/$2M) and Physical Damage Insurance ($250K minimum). Email your signed certificate to mammothemail@mammothmachinery.com.

Download Insurance Template
04

Start Renting

Once your documentation is received and processed, your customer file is set up and you can begin renting immediately. Call us at 801-295-3302 to schedule your first rental.

FAQ

Common Questions

My Rental Won't Start / Is Giving Errors

Call your sales rep immediately. We'll dispatch a technician to your job site to troubleshoot, or we'll swap the unit out so your project stays on schedule. Our goal is to minimize your downtime.

Calling Off & Returning Your Rental

When you're done with your rental, call or text your sales rep to schedule a pickup or coordinate a return to our yard. We handle transport so you can focus on your project.

Accounts Payable / Invoicing

We accept ACH, credit card, and check payments. For billing questions, call our office at 801-295-3302 or email inquiries@mammothmachinery.com.

Lien Notice

Standard pre-lien and lien notices are sent as part of our standard business process. This is routine for the industry — there is no upfront deposit required to rent with Mammoth.
