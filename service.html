Service That Keeps You Moving

Mammoth Machinery now offers field service, repair, and maintenance. Our team of technicians and field service personnel are experienced and committed to solving your needs quickly and efficiently.

We use factory OEM parts whenever possible, so repairs are done with the right components, not cheap substitutes. If a job needs something specialized, we'll tell you up front and help coordinate the next best step. And if we can't fix it on site we will coordinate transportation to and from our shop.

WHAT WE DO

Our Services

Preventive Maintenance

Scheduled maintenance programs to keep your equipment running reliably and prevent costly breakdowns before they happen.

Engine & Drivetrain Repair

Complete engine diagnostics, rebuilds, and drivetrain service. From minor repairs to major overhauls, we handle it all.

Hydraulic System Service

Hydraulic line repair, pump rebuilds, cylinder resealing, and complete system diagnostics to restore full operating pressure.

Electrical Diagnostics

Advanced electrical system troubleshooting, wiring harness repair, and ECM diagnostics using factory-level tools.

Undercarriage Work

Track replacement, roller rebuilds, sprocket and idler service for dozers, excavators, and compact track loaders.

Welding & Fabrication

Structural welding, bucket repair, wear plate installation, and custom fabrication for attachments and components.

Field Service & Emergency Repair

Our mobile technicians come to your job site for on-site repairs. Minimize downtime with rapid-response field service.
